Welcome to a summery of the Gentoo distro

Gentoo is known for its high level of customization and performance-focused design. Most software is compiled directly on the user's machine, allowing features and optimizations to be tailored to specific hardware and requirements. Gentoo gives users fine-grained control over their system through its Portage package management system and USE flags, which determine which features are included when software is built. This flexibility makes it popular among enthusiasts, developers, and users who want complete control over their environment.
